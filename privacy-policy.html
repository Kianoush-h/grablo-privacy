Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

Welcome to Grablo ("we", "our", "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it.

Summary: Grablo helps you scan grocery receipts, track your pantry items, and cook together with friends to reduce food waste. We only collect the data necessary to provide these features.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Name, email address, and username when you create an account
  • Password (stored securely using industry-standard hashing)

Pantry & Grocery Data

  • Grocery items extracted from scanned receipts or photos
  • Item names, quantities, categories, and expiry dates
  • Trip/list names and organization preferences

Social Features Data

  • Friends list and friend requests
  • Cooking session data (recipes, preferences, ratings) shared between friends

Device Permissions

  • Camera: Used to scan receipts and food items. Photos are processed to extract grocery information and are not stored beyond processing.
  • Photo Library: Used to select existing photos of receipts for scanning.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App usage data and crash reports
  • Advertising identifiers (for serving ads)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Grablo app
  • Process and analyze receipt images to extract grocery data
  • Track pantry items and send expiry reminders
  • Enable social cooking features with friends
  • Generate recipe suggestions based on your available ingredients
  • Send notifications about friend requests, cooking invitations, and expiring items
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Analyze app usage to improve user experience

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For authentication, database storage, and real-time features. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Microsoft Azure OpenAI: For AI-powered receipt scanning and recipe suggestions. Your ingredient data may be processed by Azure AI services. Microsoft Privacy Statement
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data. Google Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on cloud servers provided by Supabase. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure password hashing
  • Row-level security policies on database tables
  • Access controls and authentication requirements

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in these cases:

  • With your friends: When you use social cooking features, your ingredient data and cooking preferences are shared with the friends you choose to cook with.
  • With service providers: Third-party services listed above that help us operate the app.
  • For legal reasons: If required by law, regulation, or legal process.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal obligations.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.

8. Children's Privacy

Grablo is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

9. Advertising

Grablo displays advertisements through Google AdMob. These ads may use device advertising identifiers and usage data to show relevant advertisements. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy within the app or by sending you a notification. Your continued use of Grablo after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
